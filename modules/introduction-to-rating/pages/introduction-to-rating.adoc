#  Introduction to the Rating Service

The Rating Service (CloudKitty) available with RHOSO Observability provides chargeback and rating capabilities to RHOSO clouds. This service fills the gap between your raw technical metrics and your financial operations. 

Key points:

*   CloudKitty bridges raw OpenStack telemetry data with enterprise FinOps middleware, providing structured and reliable data for financial analysis.
*   It leverages the Telemetry Operator to gather necessary data for its rating and chargeback calculations.
*   It has been added to RHOSO Observability since RHOSO 18 FR5.
