#  Hands-on Lab: Implementing Your First Chargeback Rating

This hands-on lab provides practical experience in configuring and using the Rating service. You will use Ansible playbooks from the https://github.com/opentraining-labs-customizations/rhoso-chargeback[lab repository] along with the concepts from the training to complete the following activities:

* xref:setting-up-the-lab-environment-for-chargeback.adoc[Set up the lab environment for chargeback] by installing the required dependencies and enabling the Rating service in RHOSO.
* xref:creating-and-applying-a-rating-rule.adoc[Create and apply a rating rule] for a compute flavor using the hashmap module.
* xref:verifying-and-analyzing-consumption-data.adoc[Verify and analyze consumption data] by validating your rating configuration.
